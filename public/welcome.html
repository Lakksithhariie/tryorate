Getting Started

  1. Click the Orate icon in your Chrome toolbar
  2. Enter your email to receive a magic link
  3. Submit 3-10 writing samples (1,500+ words)
  4. Build your voice profile
  5. Select text on any page and press Ctrl+Shift+O
  6. Get voice-matched rewrites instantly!
